More

* Project `Carambole <http://morpheo.inrialpes.fr/carambole/>`_: Our project with F. Hétroy-Wheeler and J. Pansiot to capture plant motions in 3D
* GDR `PhyP <https://gdrphyp.wordpress.com/>`_: group of researchers interested in the Biophysics and Biomechanics of plants
* GDR `MéPhy <https://blog.espci.fr/mephy/>`_: group of researchers interested in the Physics and Mechanics of Complex Systems
* `Plants in Motion <http://plantsinmotion.bio.indiana.edu/>`_ : a collection of beautiful movies by Roger Hangarter
